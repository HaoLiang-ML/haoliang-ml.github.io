Hao Liang is a Research Associate at King's College London, working closely with Yali Du (KCL) and Biwei Huang (UCSD). He received his PhD from The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), Shenzhen, under the supervision of Zhi-Quan (Tom) Luo. His research lies at the intersection of statistical and computational efficiency in decision-making algorithms, with a particular focus on risk awareness, safety, and multi-agent systems. He is also interested in exploring the applications of these algorithms in various domains, including large language models (LLMs), energy system, and robotics.
