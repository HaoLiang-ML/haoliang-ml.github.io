Hao Liang is a final-year Ph.D. candidate at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), Shenzhen, China. He is advised by Zhi-Quan (Tom) Luo. His research focuses on developing algorithms that are both statistically and computationally efficient for decision-making, with a particular emphasis on risk-awareness and multi-agent systems. He is also interested in exploring the applications of these algorithms in various domains, including generative AIs, microgrids, and wireless communications.
