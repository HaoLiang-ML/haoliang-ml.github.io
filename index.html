Hao Liang is a Research Associate at King's College London. He received his PhD from The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), Shenzhen, under the supervision of Zhi-Quan (Tom) Luo. His research lies at the intersection of statistical and computational efficiency in decision-making algorithms, with a particular focus on risk awareness, safety, and multi-agent systems. He is also interested in exploring the applications of these algorithms in various domains, including LLMs, energy systems, and robotics.
